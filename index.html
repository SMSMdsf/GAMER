🚨 OPERATION: SKITTLINI BLACK OPS 🚨

THE SITUATION: Following the chaotic events of the Skittlini Outbreakini, your friend group got split up and trapped deep inside a hostile alien-infested hot zone. The mutant crawlers are multiplying rapidly!

YOUR MISSION: You are Operator Shuceeb. You must deploy solo into the grid, hunt down the invading creatures, and locate your 5 trapped companions to form the ultimate rolling firing squad.

⌨️ TACTICAL CONTROLS:

  • W, A, S, D - Move through the battlefield map
  • MOUSE CURSOR - Aim your weapon crosshair 360 degrees
  • LEFT MOUSE CLICK - Hold down to spray ammo ammunition
  • R KEY - Trigger manual magazine reload sequence
  • CYAN CRATES - Run over glowing weapon drops to unlock the Remington Shotgun or .50 Cal Sniper
